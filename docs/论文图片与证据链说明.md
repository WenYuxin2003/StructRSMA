## Figure 1. 方法流程图

文件：

```text
docs/figures/fig_method_overview.png
docs/figures/fig_method_overview.pdf
```

论文中作用：

这张图用于解释本文方法的整体流程。它说明我们不是改变 DeepRSMA 的下游 affinity 数据集，而是在 pKd fine-tuning 之前加入一个 PDB-derived nucleotide-atom contact pretraining 阶段。

建议图注：

```text
Figure 1. Overview of the contact-supervised DeepRSMA framework. RNA-ligand complexes from PDB are converted into nucleotide-atom contact maps using a 4 Angstrom distance cutoff. The DeepRSMA backbone is first pretrained to predict contact maps and then transferred to R-SIM affinity prediction, where the model is fine-tuned with pKd regression loss.
```

它支撑的论点：

```text
本文方法是一个结构监督预训练框架，而不是简单改动 affinity head。
```

## Figure 7. Shuffled contact label 对照图

文件：

```text
docs/figures/fig_contact_shuffle_control.png
docs/figures/fig_contact_shuffle_control.pdf
```

论文中作用：

这张图是关键 negative control，用来证明性能提升来自真实 nucleotide-atom contact 结构，而不是简单地多用了 PDB RNA-ligand 样本。shuffled-label 对照在每个样本内部随机打乱 contact map，保留 positive contact 数量和 contact density，但破坏具体 nucleotide-atom 对应关系。

核心结果：

```text
True contact pretraining:
mean top-k precision = 0.3471
AUPRC = 0.2847
AUROC = 0.8828

Shuffled contact pretraining:
mean top-k precision = 0.0261
AUPRC = 0.0571
AUROC = 0.7327

True validation contact density = 0.0211
```

建议图注：

```text
Figure 7. Shuffled-label control for contact-supervised pretraining. Contact labels were randomly permuted within each RNA-ligand complex while preserving the number of positive contacts. When evaluated against true contact labels, the shuffled-label checkpoint shows a near-baseline mean top-k precision of 0.0261, whereas true contact pretraining reaches 0.3471, supporting that the learned signal depends on physically meaningful nucleotide-atom correspondence.
```

它支撑的论点：

```text
真实 contact label 中的 nucleotide-atom 对应关系是有效监督信号；下游提升不只是因为额外 PDB 数据、额外训练步数或 contact density 本身。
```

```text
PCC  = 0.5738 +/- 0.0495

RMSE = 0.8665 +/- 0.0400
```

建议图注：

```text
Figure 8. Downstream shuffled-contact control on independent-test affinity prediction. Under the best-RMSE selection protocol, shuffled-contact pretraining fails to improve over the reproduced DeepRSMA baseline, whereas true contact pretraining improves PCC and SCC and reduces RMSE. This supports that the downstream gain is driven by physically meaningful contact supervision rather than additional PDB pretraining alone.
```

它支撑的论点：

```text
真实 contact map 的结构对应关系不仅提升 contact prediction 本身，也能迁移到最终 pKd prediction；随机化 contact label 不能产生同样的 downstream gain。
```

## 当前证据链

现在论文的证据链可以组织成：

```text
1. 方法层面：
   PDB structures -> distance-derived contact labels -> contact-supervised pretraining -> affinity fine-tuning

2. 数据层面：
   Contact500 比 Contact100 有更多 PDB complex、ligand type、positive contact 和 nucleotide-atom pair

3. 预训练层面：
   contact top-k precision 明显高于 contact density baseline，说明模型学到了接触关系

4. 下游任务层面：
   Contact500SkipAff 在 independent test 上显著超过原始 DeepRSMA

5. 规模效应层面：
   0 -> 94 -> 484 contact samples 对应 affinity performance 逐步提升

6. 可解释性层面：
   contact map 示例显示模型能定位真实接触区域

7. Negative-control 层面：
   shuffled contact label 在 contact task 和 downstream pKd task 上都不能复现真实 contact pretraining 的提升
```
